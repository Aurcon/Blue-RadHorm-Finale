In the 1940s, it was observed by researchers that radiation workers showed lower cancer rates than expected, challenging the linear no-threshold (LNT) model of radiation protection.

What is Radiation Hormesis?

The Core Concept

Radiation hormesis is the concept that low doses of ionizing radiation may stimulate or induce protective mechanisms in cells. Rather than claiming that radiation is safe, it suggests that dose given and context matter, with low-dose exposure potentially triggering repair, immune support, and stress resistance.

How It Works

🛡️

Adaptive Protection

Low levels of radiation may trigger cellular dfenses like a release of antioxidants or DNA repair activation.

📈

U-Shaped Response

This concept has a model wherein low doses may be beneficial, neutral effect near the threshold, and harm at high doses or above threshold.

🌍

Natural Radiation

All life is exposed to background radiation everyday ranging from cosmic rays, soil minerlas, and radon.
